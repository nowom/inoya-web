Not an app.
An ecosystem of states.

INOYA doesn't describe a function — it invites an experience. Inspired by the Japanese concepts of inochi (life), inori (intention), and ya (a place of shelter), INOYA is a home for what is alive and meaningful in you.

It's a name that lives alongside its user — growing in meaning as you grow in awareness.

Five pillars

One space, many states of being

Each pillar responds to a different emotional need — never mixing rest with growth, being with doing, silence with analysis.

🌿

Memories

"What I have lived"

The heart of INOYA. A timeline and map of your moments — no judgement, no goals. Just life as it is, beautifully preserved.

🌊

Affirmative Space

"I am here. I need nothing."

A lean-back space for emotional regulation. Gentle affirmations, soft music, no tasks. Just being.

☀️

Practice

"I want to learn or do something"

Breathing exercises, reflective prompts, quizzes, and knowledge. An intentional space for growth and self-awareness.

🌸

Affirmation Mirror

"I see myself"

An intimate space with your camera and reflection. Affirmations respond to your emotions — no analysis, just presence.

🧠

AI Coach

"Someone guides me"

Personalised dialogue, reflections, and suggestions. AI that speaks the language of affirmation, not analysis.
