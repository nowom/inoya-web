Why it feels different

One simple ritual to help you come back to yourself.

Inoya is designed around one clear experience. It does not ask you to manage more, track more, or do more. It helps you pause, soften the noise, and find your next small step.

01

Pause

The experience begins with breath, not pressure. A calm first moment helps the mind slow down before reflection begins.

02

Release

What feels heavy can be named in simple words. No perfect journaling. No performance. Just space to let it out.

03

Move gently

The goal is not a plan for everything. It is one possible step that feels kind, clear, and doable today.

How it works

A gentle flow from overwhelm to clarity.

Each reset follows the same calm sequence, helping the user move from tension and noise toward steadiness and direction.

1. Breathe

A short breathing moment helps regulate the body before the mind starts sorting through what feels heavy.

2. Let it out

Everything can come out in simple language. The user does not need to organize it yet.

3. Focus

From all the noise, one thing begins to stand out as the most important right now.

4. Take one small step

Instead of solving everything, the user chooses one possible action for today.

5. Close softly

The reset ends with a moment of calm, leaving the user with less pressure and more clarity.

FAQ

Questions, answered simply.

Is Inoya a therapy app?

No. Inoya is a self-guided reset experience for calming internal tension and finding clarity in a short moment.

Can I change the colors later?

Yes. The key colors are defined in CSS variables at the top of style.css, so the brand palette can be updated quickly.

Does this version work for a pre-launch landing page?

Yes. It avoids talking about product changes and focuses on the value the user gets right now.
